Update

To update Gishiki you need to follow some simple steps:
  • Upload the new version of Gishiki on your website, where the old version was located, overwriting the old version, except config.php
  • Direct your browser to index.php, the installer/updater will be launched
  • Follow on-screen instructions (if any)
Your version of Gishiki is now updated. Everything will work exactly like before, but you had got bug fixes and/or new features for free (just a few minutes :) ).
